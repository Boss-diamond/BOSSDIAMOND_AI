About BossDiamond AI

BossDiamond AI is a smart document summarization assistant powered by advanced AI. It allows users to upload PDF, DOCX, or TXT files and instantly get a concise summary.

This application is designed to provide a clean, intuitive interface so users can focus on their content without distractions.

Our goal is to make AI-powered summarization accessible to everyone, with seamless chat interaction and file handling.
